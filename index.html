About

Being an entrepreneur is indeed quite intimidating. It’s a journey full of constant hurdles with unique problems hitting you swiftly without any warning. Managing the personal drain on your mind is as important as solving these problems. Knowing that you aren’t alone is a comforting feeling, but also surrounding yourself with like-minded smart people can be a differentiating factor. C-Suites is that community of like-minded people within VNIT Nagpur where we grow ourselves individually along with the people around us.
